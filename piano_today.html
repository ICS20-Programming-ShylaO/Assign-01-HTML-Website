The Current Piano

Over the centuries, the piano has changed drastically in accordance with the new innovative technology. Now, a lot of pianos are digital, using electricity. Going further with the idea of digital pianos, there are applications on different devices that mimic the piano. For example, GarageBand is an app on your phone where you can record or play (using the software) all sorts of musical instruments, including the piano.
